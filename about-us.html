Dining

Enjoy Delicious Meals At AP Palace, we offer a wide range of tasty dishes from local favorites to international cuisine. Our dining area provides a cozy and elegant atmosphere, perfect for enjoying a meal with family or friends.

Banquet

Celebrate Your Events Our spacious banquet hall is perfect for weddings, parties, or corporate events. Located near the Ram Mandir, it offers a beautiful setting for all your special occasions..

confrence Hall

Perfect for Meetings Our modern conference hall is ideal for business meetings, seminars, and small events. Equipped with Wi-Fi and the latest technology, it’s a great space for your professional needs in Ayodhya.

Welcome to AP Palace & Hotel, a sanctuary of comfort and elegance in the historic city of Ayodhya. Nestled in the peaceful locality of Devkali, Faizabad, our hotel is designed to offer you a luxurious stay combined with the warmth of traditional Indian hospitality. Whether you're visiting for pilgrimage, leisure, or business, AP Palace & Hotel is the ideal choice for your stay. We are located just a short distance from iconic landmarks such as Ram Janmabhoomi, Hanuman Garhi, and the serene Saryu River, allowing easy access to Ayodhya’s spiritual and cultural heritage.
